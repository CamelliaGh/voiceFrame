## Influencer Partnership Strategy

### Micro-Influencer Strategy (1k-50k followers)

#### Target Niches
1. **Wedding Content Creators**: Wedding planners, brides, wedding photographers
2. **Parenting Influencers**: New moms, parenting bloggers, family content creators
3. **Gift/Lifestyle Bloggers**: Gift guides, lifestyle influencers, home decor
4. **DIY/Craft Influencers**: Craft bloggers, DIY enthusiasts, creative content

#### Partnership Models
1. **Gifting + Affiliate**: Free product + 20% commission on sales
2. **Discount Code**: Unique discount code for followers (15% off)
3. **Story Takeover**: Authentic showcasing in Instagram Stories
4. **Giveaway Collaborations**: Joint giveaways with other brands

#### Outreach Template
```
Hi [Name],

I love your content about [specific post/theme]. I run VoiceFrame,
where we turn audio memories (like wedding songs, baby's first words)
into beautiful posters.

I'd love to gift you a custom poster in exchange for an honest review.
Plus, I can offer your followers 15% off with code [NAME15].

Interested? I think your audience would love this!

Best,
[Your name]
```

#### Success Metrics
- **Partnerships**: 10-15 micro-influencers per month
- **Conversions**: Track via unique discount codes
- **ROI**: Calculate CPM and ROI per partnership
- **Relationships**: Build long-term relationships with top performers

## Social Media Advertising Strategy

### Facebook/Instagram Ads

#### Campaign Structure
1. **Awareness Campaign (40% budget)**
   - Objective: Reach
   - Targeting: Interests (gift giving, personalization, weddings, new parents)
   - Creative: Emotional transformation videos
   - Placement: Instagram feed, Stories, Facebook feed

2. **Consideration Campaign (30% budget)**
   - Objective: Traffic
   - Targeting: Website visitors (retargeting)
   - Creative: Customer testimonials, product benefits
   - Placement: All placements

3. **Conversion Campaign (30% budget)**
   - Objective: Purchase
   - Targeting: Preview creators, cart abandoners
   - Creative: Urgency, limited-time offers
   - Placement: Instagram feed, Facebook feed

#### Ad Creative Testing
- **A/B Test**: Emotional vs. Product-focused
- **Format Test**: Video ads vs. Carousel ads
- **Content Test**: Customer UGC vs. Professional shots
- **Audience Test**: Different occasions (anniversary vs. baby vs. wedding)

#### Targeting Strategy
1. **Phase 1: Warm Audiences**
   - Website visitors (last 30 days)
   - Video viewers (75%+)
   - Instagram engagers

2. **Phase 2: Lookalike Audiences**
   - 1% lookalike of customers
   - 1% lookalike of preview creators
   - 1-2% lookalike of website visitors

3. **Phase 3: Interest-Based**
   - Etsy shoppers + personalized gifts
   - Recently engaged + gift giving
   - New parents + memory keeping
   - Anniversary + romantic gifts

## Crisis Management

### Potential Issues
1. **Negative Comments**: Customer complaints, product issues
2. **Copyright Issues**: Music usage, image rights
3. **Privacy Concerns**: Customer data, audio content
4. **Competitor Attacks**: Negative reviews, false claims

### Response Strategy
1. **Monitor Mentions**: Use social listening tools
2. **Quick Response**: Respond within 2 hours
3. **Take Conversations Private**: Move to DM when appropriate
4. **Document Everything**: Keep records of interactions
5. **Escalate When Needed**: Involve management for serious issues

### Prevention Measures
1. **Clear Guidelines**: Establish social media policies
2. **Training**: Educate team on best practices
3. **Approval Process**: Review content before posting
4. **Legal Review**: Ensure compliance with regulations
5. **Regular Audits**: Monitor content and performance
