## Budget Reallocation Rules

### Winning Campaigns
- **Increase Budget**: 20-50% increase for campaigns with ROAS >2.0
- **Scale Gradually**: Increase budget by 20% weekly for consistent performers
- **Monitor Performance**: Watch for performance degradation at higher spend
- **Set Limits**: Maximum 200% of original budget for any single campaign

### Losing Campaigns
- **Decrease Budget**: 50% decrease for campaigns with ROAS <1.5
- **Pause Campaigns**: Pause campaigns with ROAS <1.0 for 7+ days
- **Analyze Issues**: Identify and fix issues before restarting
- **Test Variations**: Test new creative, targeting, or landing pages

### Break-Even Campaigns
- **Maintain Budget**: Keep budget stable for campaigns with ROAS 1.5-2.0
- **Optimize Further**: Focus on optimization before scaling
- **Test Improvements**: A/B test creative, targeting, landing pages
- **Monitor Trends**: Watch for improvement or degradation trends

### New Tests
- **Allocate 10-20%**: Reserve 10-20% of budget for new tests
- **Test Small**: Start with small budgets for new campaigns
- **Scale Winners**: Scale successful tests quickly
- **Kill Losers**: Stop unsuccessful tests quickly
