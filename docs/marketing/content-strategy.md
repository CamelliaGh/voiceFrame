### Content Creation Guidelines

#### Visual Content Requirements
- **High-quality images**: 1080x1080 for Instagram, 2:3 ratio for Pinterest
- **Consistent branding**: Purple color scheme, warm tones
- **Emotional focus**: People, families, gift-giving moments
- **Clear CTAs**: Visible, compelling call-to-action buttons

#### Writing Guidelines
- **Emotional language**: Focus on feelings and memories
- **Benefit-focused**: What the customer gets, not just features
- **Conversational tone**: Warm, approachable, not corporate
- **SEO-optimized**: Natural keyword integration
- **Mobile-friendly**: Short paragraphs, scannable content

#### Content Performance Metrics
- **Blog posts**: Organic traffic, time on page, conversion rate
- **Social media**: Engagement rate, reach, click-through rate
- **Email**: Open rate, click rate, conversion rate
- **Overall**: Brand awareness, customer acquisition cost
