## Email Segmentation Strategy

### Segmentation Criteria
1. **Purchase History**: First-time buyers, repeat customers, high-value customers
2. **Engagement Level**: Active subscribers, inactive subscribers, new subscribers
3. **Demographics**: Age, gender, location (if available)
4. **Behavior**: Cart abandoners, email openers, social media engagers
5. **Preferences**: Template preferences, occasion interests, communication frequency

### Segment-Specific Campaigns

#### First-Time Buyers
- **Focus**: Education, onboarding, building trust
- **Content**: How-to guides, customer stories, product education
- **Frequency**: Weekly for first month, then monthly

#### Repeat Customers
- **Focus**: Loyalty, upselling, cross-selling
- **Content**: New templates, special offers, loyalty rewards
- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly

#### High-Value Customers
- **Focus**: VIP treatment, exclusive offers, early access
- **Content**: Premium content, exclusive discounts, new product previews
- **Frequency**: Weekly

#### Inactive Subscribers
- **Focus**: Re-engagement, win-back campaigns
- **Content**: Special offers, new features, customer stories
- **Frequency**: Monthly

## Email Design Guidelines

### Visual Design
- **Brand Colors**: Purple primary, warm accent colors
- **Typography**: Clean, readable fonts
- **Images**: High-quality, emotional, gift-focused
- **Layout**: Mobile-responsive, scannable
- **CTAs**: Clear, prominent, action-oriented

### Content Guidelines
- **Subject Lines**: Emotional, benefit-focused, under 50 characters
- **Preheader Text**: Compelling, action-oriented, under 100 characters
- **Body Content**: Conversational, benefit-focused, scannable
- **CTAs**: Action-oriented, benefit-focused, prominent

### Mobile Optimization
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for all screen sizes
- **Touch-Friendly**: Large buttons, easy navigation
- **Fast Loading**: Optimized images, minimal code
- **Readable**: Large fonts, short paragraphs

## Email Performance Metrics

### Key Metrics
1. **Open Rate**: Target 25-35% (industry average: 20-25%)
2. **Click Rate**: Target 5-8% (industry average: 2-3%)
3. **Conversion Rate**: Target 2-5% (industry average: 1-2%)
4. **Unsubscribe Rate**: Target <0.5% (industry average: 0.5-1%)
5. **Bounce Rate**: Target <2% (industry average: 2-5%)

### Campaign-Specific Goals
- **Welcome Series**: 40% open rate, 15% click rate
- **Abandoned Cart**: 30% open rate, 10% click rate, 5% conversion
- **Post-Purchase**: 35% open rate, 8% click rate
- **Seasonal Campaigns**: 30% open rate, 12% click rate, 3% conversion

### A/B Testing Strategy
1. **Subject Lines**: Test emotional vs. benefit-focused
2. **Send Times**: Test morning vs. evening sends
3. **Content Length**: Test short vs. long content
4. **CTA Buttons**: Test color, text, placement
5. **Images**: Test lifestyle vs. product images

## Email Tools and Automation

### Recommended Tools
1. **Mailchimp**: Free up to 500 contacts, good automation
2. **Klaviyo**: Advanced segmentation, e-commerce focused

### Automation Workflows
1. **Welcome Series**: 5-email sequence over 7 days

3. **Post-Purchase**: 4-email sequence over 3 months
4. **Seasonal Campaigns**: 4-email sequence over 6-8 weeks

### Integration Requirements
- **E-commerce Platform**: Order data, customer information

## Compliance and Best Practices

### Legal Compliance
1. **CAN-SPAM Act**: Clear sender identification, unsubscribe option
2. **GDPR**: Consent-based marketing, data protection
3. **CASL**: Canadian anti-spam legislation compliance
4. **Privacy Policy**: Clear data usage, storage, sharing policies

### Best Practices
1. **Permission-Based**: Only email subscribers who opted in
2. **Value-First**: Provide value before asking for sales
3. **Frequency**: Respect subscriber preferences, don't over-email
4. **Personalization**: Use subscriber data for relevant content
5. **Testing**: A/B test subject lines, content, send times
6. **Monitoring**: Track performance, adjust based on data
7. **Clean Lists**: Regular list cleaning, remove inactive subscribers
8. **Segmentation**: Send relevant content to specific segments

### Deliverability Best Practices
1. **Authentication**: SPF, DKIM, DMARC records
2. **Reputation**: Maintain good sender reputation
3. **Content**: Avoid spam triggers, maintain good content
4. **Engagement**: Encourage opens, clicks, replies
5. **List Quality**: Clean lists, engaged subscribers
6. **Frequency**: Don't over-email, respect preferences
7. **Monitoring**: Track deliverability metrics, adjust as needed
